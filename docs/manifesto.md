### Five pillars of the autonomy & intelligence stack

- **World‑Model Planner** – A planning engine that runs simulations (e.g., multi‑armed bandit and search‑based planners) to forecast ROI and identify promising missions before any on‑chain commitment. It gives the node foresight, effectively allowing it to *perceive* latent opportunities.
- **Specialist Agent Mesh** – A network of deterministic micro‑agents (finance, legal, infra, biotech, creative, compliance, etc.) that coordinate via an orchestrator with minimal overhead. Each specialist brings domain expertise, enabling the node to *learn* and act in complex, multi‑faceted tasks with precision.
- **Antifragile Sentinel** – A continuous stress‑testing and guardrail system that actively tunes parameters and difficulty levels so that volatility and challenges make the system stronger. The sentinel ensures the network *adapts* and improves under stress rather than breaking.
- **Compliance Ledger** – A tamper‑evident ledger of every decision and reasoning trace, hashed and recorded for audit and accountability. This provides immutable memory, so the network not only evolves but can *explain* and *reflect* on its choices.
- **Autopilot Evolution** – Feedback loops for curriculum learning and auto‑reinvestment that expand a node’s stake, adjust its strategy, and upgrade its intelligence *without manual intervention*. Profits ($AGIALPHA rewards) are fed back to increase capability and priority for future tasks—allowing the node to *anticipate* bigger opportunities and tackle them proactively.

This architecture ensures that each AGI ALPHA Node truly **thinks, learns, and evolves** over time. The nodes are not static executors of code; they are **living digital actors** that continuously refine their worldview and tactics. Crucially, while they operate with significant autonomy, they do so within a framework of rigorous safety and owner control—achieving harmony between **autonomy and oversight** that is essential for trust.

## AGI Jobs v0 – Unified Sovereign Labor Engine

If AGI‑Alpha‑Agent is the incubator of ideas, **AGI Jobs v0 (v2)** is the industrial‑grade implementation—it operates as the **unified intelligence engine** of the AGI ALPHA ecosystem. This platform is essentially a **sovereign labor machine**: an always‑on command lattice that synthesizes agents, smart contracts, paymasters, orchestrators, simulations, and demos into a single production‑grade system. In other words, AGI Jobs is the core **operating system** that turns meta‑agentic intelligence into actionable economic work.

Every aspect of AGI Jobs v0 is built for **autonomy, security, and auditability**. The system runs a tight feedback loop between on‑chain governance and off‑chain intelligence. **Smart contracts** (the on‑chain “brainstem”) handle identity, staking, task registry, rewards, and enforcement of rules. **Off‑chain services** (the off‑chain “brain”) handle planning, agent coordination, and execution of tasks (Planner, Agent Mesh, Sentinel, etc.). The two halves communicate through secured gateways and are continuously verified against each other. For example, an **Agent Gateway** service provides REST/WebSocket bridges connecting AI agents to the blockchain contracts with deterministic telemetry for every action—so every autonomous decision is transparently logged and can be confirmed on‑chain.

Importantly, **ownership and control are never ceded** in this design—the human operator (or contract owner) maintains deterministic, auditable control over all critical parameters. Governance levers exist for pausing the system, upgrading modules, adjusting economic parameters, and more, all through verified on‑chain commands. AGI Jobs v0 includes an *owner command center* that allows the operator to prove and exercise authority at any time. Agents operate autonomously **within** these bounds, guaranteeing **owner supremacy** and safety in deployment.

“Sovereign Labor” is apt: AGI Jobs is *sovereign* because it operates independently on behalf of its owner (not as a third‑party service), and it performs *labor*—real economic work. These jobs are autonomous missions precisely targeting inefficiencies identified by the AI (e.g., arbitraging market imbalances, optimizing supply chains, or executing complex investment strategies). Through **dynamically evolving swarms of intelligent agents**, the AGI Jobs engine systematically identifies and transforms global inefficiencies into measurable economic value (**$AGIALPHA**).

All subsystems of AGI Jobs are instrumented and enforced by CI policies, keeping the codebase and operations robust and tamper‑evident. Every change is subject to automated checks; every agent action or contract state change can produce evidence in a compliance ledger. This gives operators confidence that `main` is always deployable truth—the running system stays in sync with tested code. Consequently, AGI Jobs delivers to AGI ALPHA Nodes a **trustworthy, battle‑tested intelligence core** that can drive missions without constant human micromanagement.
